CyberSurge: Freshers' Gauntlet

Introduction

So what is this? Some super secret hacking competition? Nope xD just a little something to help you pass time while you get settled here. On the way you'll learn a bit about the exciting world of Infosec and CTFs. Top Solvers will get a Shout Out from the Official PClub insta! But before going forward do read the instructions...

Instructions

  1. Read the challenge details thoroughly.
  2. The deadline for the challenges is 6th August EOD.
  3. Overall top 10 solvers will get a shout out from Pclub's official insta
  4. The first bloods of each of the 9 challenges will also get a shout out
  5. Please fill out the interest form before starting with the challenges.
  6. Use the provided resources to assist you in completing the challenge. There may be hidden hints so don't miss them!
  7. Submit your work using the common submission form.
  8. All the flags are of the format - pclub{some_stuff_here}
